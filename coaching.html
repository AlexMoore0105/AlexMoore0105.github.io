Coaching/Mechanics

Moira: Basic Introduction

For players that are new to the game and want an effective and relatively easy character to play, Moira is a good choice. With simple abilities, Moira is very learner friendly. Moira is often referred to as a "DPS support" because she is able to heal in long bursts, as well as dealing a lot of damage over time.

Moira's Abilities

In order to charge her biotic grasp (primary fire), or healing, Moira needs to deal damage to her opponents by using her biotic grasp (alt fire). On PC, the type of grasp can be changed by using the different mouse buttons. The left mouse is used to heal and the right mouse is used to damage.

Biotic Grasp (Primary Fire)
  • Headshot:✕
  • Healing:70 per second, then lingers to heal 51 over 3 seconds
  • Ammo per shot:20 energy per second
  • Ammo:156 biotic energy
  • Cast time:0.08 seconds for heal
  • Duration:3 seconds (remainder)
  • Max. range:15 meters. Consumes biotic energy to heal teammates in front of her.
Biotic Grasp (Alt Fire)
  • Damage:50 per second
  • Headshot:✕
  • Healing:24 per second (self)
  • Ammo:156 biotic energy
  • Ammo:∞
  • Max. range:20 meters. Saps enemies' health, healing Moira and restoring her biotic energy.
Biotic Orb
  • Damage:50 per second, up to 200
  • Headshot:✕
  • Healing:65 per second, up to 300
  • Cast time:Instantly + 0.4 s. recovery
  • Duration:7 seconds, or until depleted
  • Max. range:4 meters (damage orb), 5 meters (healing orb)
  • Projectile speed:20 meters per second, 5.5 meters per second while tethered
  • Bounces off walls
  • The orb itself pierces barriers, but does not target enemies or allies through them
  • Dealing damage with Biotic Orb restores a small amount of Biotic Energy
  • Damage Orb does not affect enemy objects
  • If amplified, only the rate of damage/healing per second is increased, not the total amount
  • Moira launches a rebounding biotic sphere; she can choose between a regeneration effect that heals the allies it passes through, or a decay effect that deals damage to enemies.
    • \
Fade
  • Duration:0.752 seconds + 0.048 s. recovery
  • Max. range:15 meters (max)
  • Move. speed:+250% buff
  • Moira becomes invisible and phased out, cleansing her of most negative status effects and making her immune to damage
  • Moira can still pick up Health Packs during Fade
  • While in Fade, Moira jumps higher than normal
  • Can be used while Coalescence is active.
  • Moira's movement speed increases while making her invulnerable to damage
Coalescence
  • Damage:70 per second
  • Headshot:✕
  • Healing:140 per second 50 per second(self)
  • Cast time:0.5 seconds
  • Duration:8 seconds
  • Max. range:30 meters
  • Move. speed:+40% buff
  • Moira passively recharges her biotic energy three times faster while using Coalescence
  • Moira can use Fade while channeling Coalescence. The beam will be temporarily disabled while in Fade
  • Line of sight blocked by terrain and terrain-like objects except for Petal Platform.
  • Moira channels a long-range beam that heals allies, damages enemies, and bypasses barriers.
